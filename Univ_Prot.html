Convenience

Transcript Protocol is a cutting-edge application for sharing high school transcripts in a convenient, verifiable, and secure manner. Our team envisions a future where guidance counselors need only upload a transcript to the blockchain once, which students can then use for the rest of their careers.

Our team is devoted to protecting the privacy and information of all of our users. Using strong encryption, we guarentee that your personal information is safe with us.
